Erica Peterson is a nationally-recognized environmental journalist living in Louisville, Ky. Her work can be heard on public radio stations in Louisville and throughout the region, as well as nationally.

Appalachia is a region that has long-struggled with environmental issues--from the effects of mining and burning coal to the legacy of now-defunct industrial sites--and Erica's coverage plays an essential role in informing listeners about the complicated relationship between energy and development decisions and the natural world.

Her stories focus on the federal, state and local energy policy decisions that affect Kentuckians, as well as the environmental consequences.
